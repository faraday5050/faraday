About Me

I am a result-driven Data Science and Machine Learning Engineer with over 3 years of experience transforming raw data into actionable insights. I specialize in building predictive models, detecting anomalies, building classification and regression models, and creating pipelines that drive business decisions. My technical toolkit includes Python, Pandas, and NumPy for data manipulation, along with Scikit-learn and TensorFlow for machine learning development.

Beyond the code, I focus on solving real problems. Whether it's improving supplier quality, analyzing geospatial voting patterns, or predicting customer behavior, I ensure every model delivers measurable value. I believe the best data scientist doesn't just build accurate models — they build long-lasting solutions that stakeholders can trust and act upon.
